About Calm AI

We're on a mission to make mental health support accessible to everyone, everywhere, at any time.

Our Story

Calm AI was founded in 2023 by a diverse team of mental health professionals, AI researchers, and people with lived mental health experiences. We recognized a critical gap in mental health care: many people struggle to access support due to cost, stigma, geographic limitations, or simply because help isn't available when they need it most.

Our founder, Jay G. Abhadra, experienced firsthand the challenges of finding consistent mental health support while working in a high-stress environment. During a particularly difficult period, she wished for a supportive companion that could be there at 3 AM when anxiety kept her awake. This personal experience, combined with her background in clinical psychology and artificial intelligence, sparked the idea for Calm AI.

After two years of research, development, and testing with mental health professionals and users from diverse backgrounds, we launched Calm AI with a clear vision: to create an AI companion that provides judgment-free support, valuable coping strategies, and a safe space for self-reflection—available to anyone with an internet connection, at any time of day or night.

Our Mission

At Calm AI, we believe that everyone deserves access to mental health support. Our mission is to leverage artificial intelligence to provide personalized, evidence-based mental health assistance that complements traditional therapy and fills the gaps between professional care.

We aim to:

• Reduce barriers to mental health support by providing an accessible, affordable solution

• Help users develop healthy coping mechanisms and emotional regulation skills

• Create a judgment-free space where people can express themselves authentically

• Connect users with professional help when needed, serving as a bridge to traditional care

• Continually evolve our AI based on the latest psychological research and user feedback

Our Approach

Calm AI uses advanced natural language processing and emotion recognition to provide personalized support. Our approach is informed by evidence-based therapeutic techniques including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), and Positive Psychology.

We collaborate with licensed mental health professionals to ensure our AI provides accurate, helpful guidance. However, we're clear about our limitations: Calm AI is designed to complement, not replace, professional mental health care. Our AI companion can provide immediate support, help users practice healthy thinking patterns, and offer coping strategies—but we always encourage seeking professional help for clinical mental health conditions.

Privacy and security are at the core of our approach. All conversations with Calm AI are encrypted end-to-end, and we never share user data with third parties. We believe that feeling safe is essential for open, honest conversations about mental health.
